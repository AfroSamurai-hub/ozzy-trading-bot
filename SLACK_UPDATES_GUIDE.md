## When You'll Receive Updates

### Individual Position Updates:
- ✅ When position reaches **+2.0% or higher** (approaching TP)
- ⚠️ When position reaches **-1.0% or lower** (approaching SL)
- ⏰ Sent immediately when threshold crossed

### Positions Summary:
- 📊 Every **5 minutes** automatically
- 📊 Shows overview of all open positions
- 📊 Highlights concerning positions (near TP/SL)

### Position Opened (existing):
- 🟢 When new position is opened
- Shows entry price, size, confidence, reason

### Position Closed (existing):
- 🟢 Take Profit hit (+3%)
- 🔴 Stop Loss hit (-1.5%)
- 🔵 Manual close (AI SELL signal)
