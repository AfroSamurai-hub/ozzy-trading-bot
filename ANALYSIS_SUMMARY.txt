## 🐛 Critical Bugs Discovered

### Bug #1: No Capital Check 🚨
**Severity:** CRITICAL
**Impact:** Opened 328 positions on $5,000 capital
**Result:** $77,000 over-leveraged
**Fix:** Add `if portfolio.capital < position_size: skip_trade()`

### Bug #2: No Max Position Limit 🚨
**Severity:** CRITICAL  
**Impact:** Unlimited positions opened
**Result:** 328 concurrent positions
**Fix:** Add `if len(open_positions) >= 20: skip_trade()`

### Bug #3: Too Aggressive ⚠️
**Severity:** HIGH
**Impact:** Opening positions too frequently
**Result:** 44 per hour, 1 every 80 seconds
**Fix:** Add 2-minute cooldown between trades

### Bug #4: P&L Display 🐛
**Severity:** LOW
**Impact:** Shows $0.00 per position (should show -$1.35)
**Result:** Confusing display, but total P&L is correct
**Fix:** Check portfolio.update_positions() calculation

### Issue #5: No Closed Trades ⚠️
**Severity:** INVESTIGATION NEEDED
**Impact:** 0 positions closed in 7.5 hours
**Possible Causes:**
- Bitcoin didn't move ±3% or ±1.5% (most likely)
- TP/SL logic not running (need to verify)
**Fix:** Add debug logging to confirm checking
